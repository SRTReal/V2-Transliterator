Tenses



Tense may be regarded as a combination of Time and Aspect
In Classical usage, there are three times - Present, Past, Future and three Aspects - Imperfect, Perfect, Aorist

The main functions of the Aspects are as follows:

Aspect What is Expressed?
Imperfect Expresses continuous or repeated action
Perfect Expresses completed action or the state resulting from completed action
Aorist Expresses momentary action or sums up a whole period in a single action
